Use My Favorite Recipes4 to:

Keep track of

  1. Which cookbook your recipe is in and the page number
  2. Or the URL to your favorite recipe
  3. A picture of the prepared dish
  4. Notes on what the heck went wrong, or what you had to change
    • Different ingredients used
    • Changes in quantity (e.g. skip the salt)

Also

  1. Rate the 'true' effort level to prepare the ingredients and cook the meal and the time it took from prep work to completion!

Technology

Recipes4 uses Angular (with TypeScript) for the web UI, while still retaining Node.js with MongoDb for the backend services.
